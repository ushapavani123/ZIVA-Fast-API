name:Plyometric Training Variation

Warm-up:
- Light jogging and skipping for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Box Jumps - 4 sets x 15 reps
2. Plyo Push-Ups - 4 sets x 12 reps
3. Jumping Lunges - 4 sets x 20 reps
4. Tuck Jumps - 4 sets x 15 reps
5. Depth Jumps - 4 sets x 10 reps

Cool Down:
- Low-intensity cardio and stretching for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 45 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 600 calories
