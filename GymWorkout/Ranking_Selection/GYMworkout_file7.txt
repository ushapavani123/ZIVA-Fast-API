name:Endurance and Stamina Circuit

Warm-up:
- Jump rope for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Circuit 1 (Repeat 3 times):
   - Jumping Lunges - 1 minute
   - Push-Ups - 1 minute
   - Plank - 1 minute
2. Circuit 2 (Repeat 3 times):
   - High Knees - 1 minute
   - Burpees - 1 minute
   - Bicycle Crunches - 1 minute

Cool Down:
- Light jogging and stretching for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 40 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 550 calories
