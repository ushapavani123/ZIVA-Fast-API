name:Flexibility Focus Routine

Warm-up:
- Joint rotations for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Dynamic Stretching Circuit:
   - Leg Swings - 3 sets x 20 reps per leg
   - Arm Circles - 3 sets x 30 seconds
   - Torso Twists - 3 sets x 20 reps
2. Yoga Poses:
   - Downward Dog - 3 sets x 30 seconds
   - Cobra Pose - 3 sets x 20 seconds
   - Child's Pose - 3 sets x 30 seconds

Cool Down:
- PNF stretching for 10 minutes

Total Workout Time- 50 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 350 calories
