name:Bodyweight Emphasis Session

Warm-up:
- Bodyweight exercises (jumping jacks, high knees) for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Push-Ups - 4 sets x 15 reps
2. Bodyweight Squats - 4 sets x 20 reps
3. Plank Variations - 4 sets x 30 seconds each
4. Reverse Lunges - 4 sets x 12 reps per leg
5. Pull-Ups (or assisted pull-ups) - 4 sets x max reps

Cool Down:
- Gentle stretching for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 50 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 450 calories
