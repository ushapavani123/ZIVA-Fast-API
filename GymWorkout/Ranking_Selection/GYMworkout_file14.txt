name:Strength and Stability Challenge

Warm-up:
- Stability ball exercises for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Stability Ball Squats - 4 sets x 15 reps
2. Plank with Leg Lifts - 4 sets x 30 seconds
3. Dumbbell Step-Ups onto Bench - 4 sets x 12 reps per leg
4. Medicine Ball Woodchops - 4 sets x 20 reps
5. TRX Suspension Push-Ups - 4 sets x 12 reps

Cool Down:
- Stability ball stretches for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 45 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 550 calories
