Exercise Name: Pilates
Description: 
Target Muscle Groups: Core, legs, glutes
Instructions:
1. Start with a mat or Pilates equipment such as a reformer or stability ball.
2. Perform exercises that focus on core stability and control.
3. Engage your abdominal muscles and maintain proper alignment throughout each movement.
4. Progress to more advanced exercises as you gain strength and flexibility.
5. Cool down with some gentle stretching and deep breathing.
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Intensity: Moderate
Benefits: Improves core strength, enhances flexibility, promotes better posture.
Calories Burned: Approximately 200-400 calories per hour.
