name:Strength and Cardio Fusion

Warm-up:
- Cardio intervals (jump rope, jogging) for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Squat to Overhead Press - 3 sets x 12 reps
2. Kettlebell Swings - 3 sets x 15 reps
3. Lateral Lunges - 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
4. Battle Rope Slams - 3 sets x 30 seconds
5. Sprint Intervals - 3 sets x 1 minute

Cool Down:
- Yoga-inspired stretches for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 40 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 500 calories
