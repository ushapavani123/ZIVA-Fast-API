name:Agility and Quickness Circuit

Warm-up:
- Agility ladder drills for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Cone Drills - 3 sets x 1 minute
2. Shuttle Runs - 3 sets x 45 seconds
3. Jumping Agility Squats - 3 sets x 20 reps
4. Speed Skaters - 3 sets x 30 seconds per side
5. Side-to-Side Hurdle Jumps - 3 sets x 15 reps

Cool Down:
- Light jogging in place and dynamic stretches for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 35 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 400 calories
