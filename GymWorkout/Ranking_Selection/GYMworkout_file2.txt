name:High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Warm-up:
- Dynamic stretching for 10 minutes

Workout:
1. Jump Squats - 4 sets x 30 seconds
2. Burpees - 4 sets x 45 seconds
3. Mountain Climbers - 4 sets x 30 seconds
4. Plank Jacks - 4 sets x 45 seconds
5. Sprints (on treadmill or outdoors) - 4 sets x 1 minute

Cool Down:
- Light jogging and static stretching for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 45 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 600 calories
