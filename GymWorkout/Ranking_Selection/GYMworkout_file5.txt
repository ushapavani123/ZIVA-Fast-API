name:Upper Body Definition Routine

Warm-up:
- Arm circles for 5 minutes

Workout:
1. Push-Ups - 3 sets x 15 reps
2. Dumbbell Rows - 3 sets x 12 reps per arm
3. Tricep Dips - 3 sets x 10 reps
4. Shoulder Press - 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Bicep Curls - 3 sets x 10 reps

Cool Down:
- Arm and shoulder stretches for 5 minutes

Total Workout Time- 40 minutes
Total Calories Burned- 350 calories
